The Recipe for a More Perfect Union

An ideal voting system has several characteristics:

  • Equality: the voters must be provided an equal voice in the election

  • Accuracy: the outcome of the election should match the collective will of the voters

  • Simplicity: the ballot, computation method and results should be easy for voters to understand and easy for election officials to process

  • Expressiveness: the ballot should permit the voter to express a nuanced opinion on the outcome, not just a choice between the "lesser evil" candidates

  • Honesty: Voters should not be punished for expressing their honest opinions, and likewise voters should not be incentivized to achieve a better outcome by voting strategically
